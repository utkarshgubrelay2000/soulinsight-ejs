About Us

Our Story


  • SOUL, a pandemic start up started by 2 qualitative research practitioners out of their home work stations and a handful of clients, with the wildly ambitious dream to transform the world of insights


  • Deciphering / unraveling what is significant, purposeful and meaningful is ever evolving calling for a sharp focus on ‘mindful’ HUMAN understanding; drawing meaning from this understanding, and bringing it alive in a simple yet interesting manner. However the world of the humans is complex, not just the qualitative one. Meaning in human life can be defined sometimes quantitatively as “infinite”, qualitatively as “deeper”, relationally as “unlimited”, temporally as “eternal” and perspectivally as “from the point of view”


  • Hence translated to building a ‘fusion’ insights studio, which integrates learnings from different data sources.

    • - In this endeavor, we are joined by different practitioners and collaborators through SOUL COLLECTIVE

    • - Whether offline/online, behavioral/attitudinal, stated/latent/observational, textual/visual) or disciplines (psychology, anthropology, sociology, neurosciences, design thinking, analytics, AI/ML, VR…)

Our Mission

We want to bring HUMAN centricity in marketing decision making for brands on their journeys to find meaning for themselves and their intended target audiences

Our Vission

We intend to create meaning for marketing decision making through mindful understanding of the HUMAN involved and get to the SOUL of the matter

IDENTITY OF THE SOUL

SOUL’s identity comes alive with its logo - an amalgamation of 3 inspiring elements coming together and indicative of the mindful transformation of the research process itself

  • 1 The Bindu painting by S H Raza

    calls attention to a dot – a zero (starting point or the end; without a value but helping frame value for everything that surrounds it) or a nucleus or the very nature of human SOUL itself. We aim to help craft the idea of value for marketing decision making just like a dot.

  • 2 The Vitruvian Man

    has many meanings- perfection of the maker, the blend of art and science for some and analogous to the universe for some. We intend to create this human centricity in insighting itself.

  • 3 The Rainbow

    signifies VIBGYOR and each color represents an association and an influence. Similarly, there is no singular (brand) truth and we want help each brand identify the most relevant and unique (brand) truth.

  • 4 The Final Logo

    has many meanings- perfection of the maker, the blend of art and science for some and analogous to the universe for some. We intend to create this human centricity in insighting itself.
