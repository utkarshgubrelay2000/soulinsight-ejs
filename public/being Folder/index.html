FAQ

Know More About Us -

1. What does SOUL do?

SOUL is a fusion marketing research agency that dabbles across qualitative and quantitative research, traditional and modern ways of research. We conduct offline and online research. We help identify, create and implement the most appropriate research solutions to marketing queries.

2. What are the markets that are covered by SOUL?

SOUL is based out of India. We have the head-office in Delhi. We can take up research assignments with pan-INDIA coverage. We are capable to take up end-to-end assignments for countries with English proficiency capabilities. We are open for different levels of engagement in markets other than India, as full service or as a consultant.

3. What do we bring to the table?

We offer a fresh perspective and approach to research. We have capabilities to conduct research whether traditional or new age. While helpful even today, the consumer has evolved beyond those old, tried and tested research methods. Rich insights can come by asking the right person and the right questions. We approach their brands as custodians and work with clients as collaborators.

4. What is qualitative and quantitative research?

Qualitative research is exploratory and in-depth deep dive into any specific topic of interest. Quantitative research is when one wants to validate learnings and findings.

5. What can I understand about a consumer through quantitative and qualitative research?

You can get to know your consumer and their decision-making. You will be able to get the answers to a consumer’s behaviour. “What” appeals to a person, “Why” it appeals to a person and therefore what you need to do in marketing to get to your target consumers.

6. What is mixed-methods or hybrid research?

Often mixed-methods involves combining approaches from different disciplines (Offline/online, behavioural/attitudinal, stated/latent/observational, textual/visual or disciplines (psychology, anthropology, sociology, neurosciences, design thinking, analytics, AI/ML, VR). Different sources can be tapped into for building a holistic understanding and complete picture that can answer the business or research questions.
